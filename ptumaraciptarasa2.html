Our Heritage Story

Rooted in tradition, elevated by excellence - discover how we preserve Indonesia's culinary heritage

Nusantara Heritage Recipes

PT Umara Cipta Rasa was established as a business unit of Umara Nikmat Boga, dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich culinary heritage of Nusantara. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, carefully maintained to ensure authenticity while meeting modern standards.

From traditional Javanese delicacies to authentic Sumatran specialties, we bring the diverse flavors of Indonesia to your special occasions. Each dish tells a story of our archipelago's rich cultural tapestry.

Our Journey Through Time

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in Indonesian heritage catering

2014 - Foundation

Umara Catering (PT. Umara Cipta Rasa) was established as a business unit of Umara Nikmat Boga, focusing on premium catering service with traditional recipes.

2015 - First Milestone

Served 100,000 plates during our first year, establishing our reputation for authentic Nusantara heritage recipes.

2017 - Wedding Excellence

Wedding Achievement - Served 25,000 guests in one night at JIEXPO, showcasing our capability for large-scale events.

2018 - Official Recognition

Selected as Official Catering to provide 5,000 meals per day, demonstrating our consistency and quality standards.

2020 - Pandemic Resilience

2.3 Million Plates served through 2018-2020. Umara Catering has already served many events, adapting to new health protocols.

2022 - Award Recognition

Official Catering for G20 - U20 and M20, Won An Award of "Kategori Jasa Boga/Catering - Jakarta Tourism Awards 2022".

2025 - Future Vision

Continuing our mission to preserve and share Nusantara Heritage recipes while expanding our reach and maintaining excellence in service.
