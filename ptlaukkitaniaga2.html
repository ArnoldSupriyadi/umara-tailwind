What is Laukita?

Laukita is a product that has been developed by prioritizing consumer practicality. Laukita used real ingredients without preservative, we freeze our food at the peak of its freshness. Sealed with a packaging that applies technology vacuum on thermo sealing machine, which covers the product completely keeping the product safe and dry. With the help of our technology Laukita was able to extend the shelf life of its products up to 1 year.
